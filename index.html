Frequently Asked Questions

Have a question? Find our most commonly asked questions below.

How do solar panels work?
Solar panels convert sunlight into electricity using photovoltaic (PV) cells. These cells generate direct current (DC) electricity, which is then converted into alternating current (AC) by an inverter for use in your home or business.
What are the benefits of going solar?
Going solar reduces your electricity bills, lowers your carbon footprint, increases energy independence, and can even increase the value of your property.
How much do solar panels cost?
The cost of solar panels depends on factors like system size, location, and equipment quality. Use our solar calculator to get a quick estimate tailored to your needs.
How long do solar panels last?
Solar panels typically last 25–30 years, with most manufacturers offering performance warranties for that period. Regular maintenance can help extend their lifespan.
Are there any government subsidies for solar installations?
Yes! The Central Government Subsidy (Central Financial Assistance or CFA) is available for grid-connected rooftop solar projects in the residential sector. Additionally, the Tamil Nadu Government offers a subsidy of ₹15,000 per kW, up to a maximum of ₹30,000, for residential solar installations. These subsidies can significantly reduce your upfront costs.
